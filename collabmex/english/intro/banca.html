    • Send OTP (One-Time Password) via Email/WhatsApp

      Demonstrate how the AI Agent can send an OTP for identity verification or transaction confirmation.

      • You can check your card balances or loan balances.
      • The AI Agent will ask you to identify yourself with your phone number; provide your cell phone number.
      • You will receive an OTP to your email and via WhatsApp.
      • Provide the OTP to the AI agent, and with this, it will identify you and provide the requested information.
      • You can validate your information on the Customer Search Portal
    • Send Account Statement via Email

      Show how the AI Agent can send the monthly account statement via Email/WhatsApp.

      • Request your account statement; it will ask you to authenticate with your phone number and the OTP sent by email/WhatsApp.
      • Once authenticated, it will send you the account statement by email and WhatsApp.
      • The AI Agent will confirm the delivery to the registered email.
  4. Escalate the interaction to the Human Agent and demonstrate simplified tasks:
    • Agent Screen Customization

      The agent interface can be customized with the company name and logo for a consistent brand experience.

    • Transcription and Summary with AI Assistant

      View the transcription of the AI Agent's interaction with the customer and visualize the conversation summary with the AI Assistant.

    • Customer Information (CRM/Database)

      The agent will have complete customer information. This information is taken from a CRM or customer database.

    • Digital Call/Interaction Control Buttons
      • Call control button functionalities:
        • Put on hold
        • Make conferences
        • Transfer calls
        • Pause recording

    • System and Web Page Integration

      The agent interface integrates relevant systems and web pages, such as the customer search portal or the ATM simulator, for efficient service.

    • Search for a customer by their ID (e.g., C01, C02, C03).
    • Observe how their personal data, cards, and loans are loaded.
    • Customer Journey
      • View the complete history of customer interactions across different channels and related activities.

      • Enter the PIN (1234) and observe how this activity is shown in the Customer Journey.
      • You can enter an invalid PIN, check your balance, or make a withdrawal. Observe these activities in the customer's Customer Journey.
    • Outbound Email

      Send emails directly from the platform for seamless customer communication (e.g., transaction confirmations, document delivery).

    • Agent Performance Reports

      The Agent will be able to access the APS widget (this information is taken from Analyzer) to view agent performance reports.

  5. Show the Supervisor experience and demonstrate real-time operational visibility.
    • Initial Dashboard

    • Agent Performance Reports

      The Supervisor will be able to access the APS widget (this information is taken from Analyzer) to view agent performance reports.
