  3. Show how the AI Agent responds and automates tasks (you can do this via phone, WhatsApp, and web chat):
    • Responses to Frequently Asked Questions
      • What are the pharmacy's opening hours?
      • Do you offer home delivery service?
      • Do you accept electronic prescriptions?
      • What payment options are available?

      Download the complete document: Download

    • Product and Medication Information
      • Paracetamol 500mg (Analgesic)
      • Vitamin C 1000mg (Supplement)
      • Amoxicillin 500mg (Hygiene)
      • Probiotics (Bottle 30 capsules)

      Download the complete product list: Download

    • Schedule Virtual Consultation

      To see how the appointment is added in real time, you can access the: Medical Appointments Dashboard

      • Ask to schedule a medical appointment.
      • The agent will ask if you are an existing customer; indicate that you are and provide your cell phone number.
      • Once identified, the agent will ask for your desired date and the reason for your appointment.
      • Confirm the appointment.
      • You will receive confirmation in your email and WhatsApp, and you will be provided with the Instant Connect link to join the virtual appointment.
      • On the Medical Appointments Dashboard, you will also find the Doctor's link to start the virtual appointment.
    • Cancel Virtual Consultation
      • Ask to cancel a medical appointment.
      • The agent will ask for your phone number; provide your cell phone number.
      • Once identified, the agent will display your medical appointment.
      • Confirm cancellation of the appointment.
    • Create or Generate a Pharmacy Order

      To see how the new order is updated in real time, you can access the: Pharmacy Orders Dashboard

      • Ask to generate an order or make a purchase of medications/products.
      • The agent will ask for your phone number; provide your cell phone number.
      • Once identified, the agent will ask you for the products/medications you wish to buy and the total price.
      • Confirm the order.
      • You will receive confirmation in your email and WhatsApp.
    • Request Order Status
      • Ask the agent for the status of an order.
      • It will ask for your phone number to validate you are an existing user and will provide you with information on all your orders.

      To validate the information, you can search for your orders on this portal: Order Inquiry Portal or on the: Pharmacy Orders Dashboard

  4. Escalate the interaction to the Human Agent and demonstrate simplified tasks:
    • Agent Screen Customization

      The agent interface can be customized with the pharmacy's name and logo for a consistent brand experience.

    • Transcription and Summary with AI Assistant

      View the transcription of the AI Agent's interaction with the customer and visualize the conversation summary with the AI Assistant.

    • Customer Information and Purchase/Medical Consultation History

      The agent will have customer information, including their purchase and appointment history, taken from a CRM or customer database.

    • Digital Call/Interaction Control Buttons
      • Call control button functionalities:
        • Put on hold
        • Make conferences
        • Transfer calls
        • Pause recording
      • For digital channels, conference and transfer buttons are included.
    • System and Web Page Integration

      The agent interface integrates relevant systems and web pages, such as the portal for searching orders or the medical appointments calendar.

    • Customer Journey and Proactive Notifications
      • View the complete history of customer interactions across different channels, order-related activities, and their medical appointments.
      • You can change the status of orders on the order dashboard (e.g., "Mark as Preparing Shipment" or "Mark as Shipped"), and this will be updated in the Customer Journey. When you change the order status, you will receive a notification via email and WhatsApp.
      • You can also schedule/cancel appointments, and this will be reflected in the Customer Journey.

      Access the Orders Dashboard and the Appointments Dashboard

    • Outbound Email

      Send emails directly from the platform for seamless customer communication, including order confirmations or appointment reminders.

    • Agent Performance Reports

      The Agent will be able to access the APS widget (this information is taken from Analyzer) to view agent performance reports.

  5. Show the Supervisor experience and demonstrate real-time operational visibility.
    • Initial Dashboard

      Shows customer interactions in different queues (orders, inquiries, medical appointments) and service level in real time.

    • Agent Monitoring

      Monitor agent activities. For voice interactions, you can listen to conversations or barge in and also listen to recordings. You can also view the customer journey.

    • Agent Performance Reports

      The Supervisor will be able to access the APS widget (this information is taken from Analyzer) to view agent performance reports.

  6. Access the reports panel to show how activity and operational efficiency are visualized.
